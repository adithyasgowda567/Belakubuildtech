Our Process

From Vision to Your Keys

A transparent, step-by-step journey — so you're always in the loop from day one to handover.

1
Initial Consultation
Schedule a free meeting with our project advisor to discuss your vision, budget & timeline.
2
Site Survey & Design
We conduct a thorough site survey and create detailed architectural plans tailored to you.
3
Permits & Approvals
We handle all BBMP approvals, legal documentation, and regulatory compliance on your behalf.
4
Construction Begins
Foundation to structure — quality materials, skilled workers, and regular progress reports.
5
Finishing & Interiors
Premium finishes, flooring, electrical, plumbing, and your chosen interior specifications.
6
Key Handover
Final walkthrough, quality inspection, documentation, and a warm handover of your dream home.
